LOVED YOUNGSTERS group mentorship is a mentorship platform designed to meet teenage needs, ranging from SPIRITUAL to EMOTIONAL and ACADEMIC needs.

What you get with LY MENTORSHIP

  • Access to LY courses
  • Weekly bible study
  • LY HOUSES - Our accountability group structure with a designated HOUSE PASTOR/MENTOR who you have access to.
  • A community of CHRISTIAN friends who are heading the same direction as you.

Signs you need to become a loved youngster.

  • You don’t know who you are.
  • You don’t know why you’re on the earth.
  • You’re dealing with trauma and pain
  • You don’t have a community of Christian friends
  • You’re struggling to pray and understand this Christian faith
  • You have questions about Jesus and His nature
  • You’re struggling to love people
  • You don’t think you’re worthy, or useful to anyone or anything.
  • You’re struggling with addictions
